About Benzodiazepines Tracker

Benzos Tracker — A Simple, Private, and Free Tool for Benzodiazepine Tracking

I’ve developed Benzos Tracker as a free, easy-to-use web application designed to help individuals track their benzodiazepine usage. Whether you're tapering, managing your current dosage, or simply want to stay organized throughout your journey — this tool was built to support you.

Key Features:

  • ✅ Clean, user-friendly interface — simple and intuitive to use
  • ✅ Secure and private — your data stays safe and fully connected to your account
  • ✅ Daily logging — easily track your benzo intake day by day
  • ✅ Progress visualization — monitor your tapering progress through graphs and summaries
  • ✅ Cross-device access — available on both desktop and mobile
  • ✅ Account-based system — securely access your data from anywhere, anytime
  • ✅ Upcoming features — including reminders, insights, and data export options

This project was originally created for my personal use while tracking my own tapering process. Over time, I realized it could be helpful to others facing similar challenges, so I decided to make it publicly available.

The software is completely free to use, ad-free, fully private, and built with real-world experience in mind.

If you find Benzos Tracker helpful, I’d greatly appreciate any feedback or suggestions that could help improve the tool further.

Thank you for visiting, and wishing you continued strength on your journey. 🙏
